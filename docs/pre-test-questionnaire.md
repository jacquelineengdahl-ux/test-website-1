## Page 1: Welcome

**Title:** Welcome to the Living with Endo test programme

**Description:**

Thank you so much for being part of this. Living with Endo is a symptom tracker built specifically for women living with endometriosis — by someone who has lived with it for decades.

The goal of the platform is to give you a private, calm space to log how you feel each day, spot patterns over time, and bring clear information to your healthcare appointments.

**Why we need you:**
This is an early version of the app, and your honest feedback will directly shape what it becomes. During this test period, we'd love you to:

- Sign up and set up your profile
- Log your symptoms daily (or as often as you can) for 2 weeks
- Explore the dashboard, log overview, and history views
- Tell us what works, what doesn't, and what's missing

There are no wrong answers. Everything you share helps.
